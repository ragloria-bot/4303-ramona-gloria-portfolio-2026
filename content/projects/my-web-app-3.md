# Project Overview
Plate Mate is a prototype app designed to enhance meal management through innovative technology. This mobile and web application provides users with a personalized experience by taking into account their preferences, dietary restrictions, and cooking skills to generate customized meal plans. Users can enter their favorite ingredients and nutritional requirements, which allows the app to recommend recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists tailored to their individual lifestyles. Additionally, Plate Mate features a food discovery section that encourages users to explore new recipes and cooking techniques, making the process of meal management more engaging. Overall, Plate Mate aims to simplify meal preparation while motivating users to embark on exciting culinary endeavors.
