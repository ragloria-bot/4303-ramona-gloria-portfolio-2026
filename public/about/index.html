About Me

Greetings, my name is Ramona Gloria. I am currently enrolled as a full-time student at Texas Tech University, where I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications. As a long-time resident of southwestern Oklahoma, I have always aspired to further my education, and I am pleased to be dedicated to investing in my personal and professional development. My background includes experience in mechanics for simulation systems within avionics, during which I worked for Flight Safety for several years. I also possess a solid foundation in warehouse logistics. My professional objective is to re-enter the field of simulator technology, leveraging my enhanced skill set in computer applications. Feel free to explore my portfolio to learn more about my projects, skills, and interests in the field of computer applications. I am enthusiastic about living in the digital age and am eager to contribute to innovative solutions in this dynamic industry.

Professional & Personal Passions#

Simulation Mechanics

One of my interests is Aviation Simulation Technology. From 2006 to 2011, I had the opportunity to travel to various locations for training and experience in this field. A memorable experience was learning to fly the KC-135 and C-5 simulators. Working on trainers for these aircraft allowed me to develop a deep understanding of their systems and operations. Additionally, I had the chance to meet many skilled professionals during my assignments.

Disney World

Traveling to different destinations has always been a passion of mine. One of the most unforgettable trips I've taken was to Disney World in Orlando, Florida. The experience was truly magical and created lasting memories for my family. From the enchanting rides to the captivating shows, every moment was filled with joy and excitement. The attention to detail and the immersive atmosphere made it a truly unforgettable experience.

Fishing

Fishing is one of my favorite pastimes, especially when I get to share the experience with my family. There's something incredibly peaceful about being out on the water, surrounded by nature, waiting for that perfect catch. It's not just about the fish; it's about the quality time spent together, the stories shared, and the memories created. Whether we're casting our lines from a boat or the shore, fishing provides a wonderful opportunity to bond and enjoy each other's company.

Renovating

Renovating homes is a hobby of mine that I truly enjoy. One of the most rewarding experiences I've had was during a trip to Freeport, Bahamas, where I participated in a community renovation project. It was fulfilling to contribute my skills and see the tangible improvements we made to the homes. This experience not only enhanced my renovation skills but also deepened my appreciation for teamwork and community spirit.
